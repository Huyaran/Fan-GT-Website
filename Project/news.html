Jul 13, 2021

Happy Birthday, Guardian Tales!! It's Guardian Tales' birthday month. so grab your party hat! To celebrate, create an ecard with any of your favorite heroes in it.

Submission Period: July 13 - July 27

Winner's Announcement: July 30

Reward Distribution: July 30

Instructions

Download any Heroes from our fankit: [HERE]

Either design your own birthday card from scratch or use any ecard website featuring your favorite hero(es).

Please submit the front cover and the inside page Write a message inside the card. It can be long or short. Up to you! :)

Upload the ecard images or the link to it to our #happybday Discord channel: [HERE]

Include your User Number and Region (NA, EU, OC, or LA). You can find your region by looking at the top right corner before you tap to start the game. You can find your User Number in the game under Options (gear icon at the top right corner) > Account Settings.
